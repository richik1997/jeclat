ABOUT US

Lending expressions to the multifarious emotions of today's youth in a competitive spirit and environment,Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College pompously presents the premier cultural fest of North Bengal,JECLAT.

"JECLAT" derives its genetics from the word "e'CLAT" which means "ecstacy". As we know,festivals offer merriment,excitement and diversion from the rigors and boredoms of campus life. Offering events varied and pleasant, this seven day extravaganza from the 27th February to to the 5th March will provide the perfect platform for students to showcase their talent in the realm of skill,art and music; making it an enjoyable experiencenot only for the participants but also for the audience. Over the years the euphoria of Jeclat has been captivated with imaginations and expectations ranging from cultural,literary and mind tricky innovative events to performabces of the country's popular artists on the Grand Social Night.
Jeclat team cordially invites you to join and witness the extravaganza
