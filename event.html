Treasure Hunt

How well do you know your college???

Since the early civilization, treasure hunts have always fascinated the human race. We have grown up listening to the legendary tales of "The Arabian Nights", "Sindbad" and also witnessed "National Treasure" breaking all the box-office records. So chuck all the story-stuff and use your basic knowledge of surveying to experience the real thrill and excitement of Treasure Hunting.

Rules:

1. Participants are required to form a group of 4
2. It will consist of two rounds
3. The basic preliminary round will consist of basic questions and answers. The duration will be one hour.
4. Eight teams would qualify for the finals who would have to search for the treasure using the given clues.
